Tiny Next is a simple task picker for people who know they need to do something, but feel stuck choosing what to do first.

Add small tasks, tap ‘Pick My Next Task,’ and Tiny Next chooses one useful next action. Mark it done, skip it, snooze it, or ask for another.

Tiny Next is not a heavy planner. It is a tiny decision helper for daily momentum.

Features:
• Add quick tasks and routines
• Pick one next task with a tap
• Organize tasks by simple categories
• Mark tasks done, skipped, or snoozed
• Track completed tasks and streaks
• Works without an account
• Stores tasks locally on your device
• Optional $2.99 premium upgrade removes ads
