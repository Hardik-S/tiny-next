# Tiny Next Privacy Policy Draft

Last updated: 2026-05-10

Tiny Next is a small Android task picker that helps users choose one manageable next task.

## Information We Store

Tiny Next is designed to work without an account. Task data is expected to stay on the user's device unless a future feature clearly states otherwise.

The app may store local app preferences, task content, progress state, and the local cache of whether the one-time premium purchase has been restored or confirmed.

## Advertising

The free version may show banner ads through Google AdMob. AdMob may collect device identifiers, approximate location, app interaction data, diagnostics, and advertising-related signals according to Google's advertising policies and SDK behavior.

Tiny Next does not include interstitial ads or rewarded ads in this MVP.

## In-App Purchases

Tiny Next offers a one-time Google Play Billing product named remove_ads_premium. Google Play processes purchases. Tiny Next stores only the local entitlement state needed to hide ads and restore premium access.

## Data Sharing

Tiny Next does not sell personal data. Data may be processed by Google services used for ads, Play Billing, app distribution, crash diagnostics if added later, and platform security.

## Children

Tiny Next is intended for a general productivity audience and is not directed at children.

## Contact

For privacy questions, use the developer contact address listed on the Google Play Store listing.
